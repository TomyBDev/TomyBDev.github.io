About Game

Viral Infiltration is a Stealth Game, You control your character with WASD with abilities click 1 to go stealth and click F to hack items and objects around the map

You've been reincarnated as a virus after being stuck by the famous truck-kun. you have the aim to infect the computers of the big evil tech company that took away your social life by overworking you. take back control. infect them.

Contribution

This was originally for a Game Jam I was doing with some friends who hadn't made any games before, I was helping them learn. But we didn't get it completed enough at the time to submit.

I handled all the gameplay programming. A couple key features I worked on was an interaction system, which was built on an interface to allow for full flexability of interactable actors, along with in world widgets visuals.

Another key feature I worked on was dynamic vision cones, this was achieved by using a real-time mesh plugin, and line tracers to map out the geometry around the player. I also did all the AI for this project.
