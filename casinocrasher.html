About Game

After years of abuse in the casinos the dice have finally come for their revenge. You play as a dice man who is rolling his way back into the casino this time getting back at the dealers and freeing his dice brother and sisters.

Created as part of the GMTK Game Jam, with the theme of 'rolling of dice'. This was created in a team of three in one weekend.

Contribution

This was the first Game Jam I've done with an 3D artist, so the environment and the player character was made by them. I made the the player and all their abilities and movement.

An interesting feature I tried was the health bar. Instead of a conventional one, I used two dice that would rotate to display the players health. And they would fall in the game world when they would reach zero.
