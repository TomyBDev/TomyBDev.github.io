About Game

A new pandemic has hit. However, its no normal virus, the toys are infecting people. You play as the sole survivor of the pandemic trapped in your house.

All your family has already been infected, so you need to escape your house. Although something strange is happening, and the house keeps spreading out, when you get close to leaving.
